What is AVaaS

Boardroom technology, billed like the utility it should be.

Most AV still gets bought like a fixed asset: a large upfront spend that starts depreciating the moment it's installed. AVaaS is closer to how you already pay for internet or software — one monthly fee, and the gear comes with it.

About Gen3

A technical team that got tired of selling boxes.

Gen3's team has spent, combined, more than thirty years designing and installing AV, IT, and unified comms systems — mostly for the kind of client who never wants to think about the equipment again once it's working.

AVaaS grew out of a complaint we kept hearing on site visits: rooms full of gear nobody on staff actually understood, purchased two budget cycles ago, with no plan for what happens when it breaks.

The systems we build now are portable and modular by default — carts and mounts you can move, not concrete you have to pour. If a room's needs change, the system changes with it instead of getting torn out.

"Nobody wants to own a fax machine. So why should you own a boardroom camera?" — roughly how this conversation starts on most site visits
